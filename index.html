TI Draft: April 12th

Beginner Friendly Session - Choose your destiny.

Naaz-Rokha Alliance

Draft Order: #1

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

The Naaz-Rokha Alliance are masters of exploration and mechs. They possess a unique ability to gain additional benefits from exploration cards and can use their mechs to roll extra dice in combat. Great for players who like finding treasures and holding strong planetary positions.

Federation of Sol

Draft Order: #2

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

The Federation of Sol are the quintessential "human" faction. They excel at flooding planets with infantry using their Orbital Drop ability and have a high command token gain. They are incredibly resilient and excellent at defending their territory, making them a very forgiving choice for new players.

The Empyrean

Draft Order: #3

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

The Empyrean are the ultimate diplomats of the void. They specialize in making deals, moving through nebulae safely, and benefiting when others move through their territory. They are perfect for a player who likes to be "the friend of the table" while slowly accumulating power through trade and exploration.

The Emirates of Hacan

Draft Order: #4

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

The masters of trade. Hacan can trade with anyone, regardless of distance, and are the only faction that can trade Action Cards. If you want to be the richest person at the table, pick the Space Lions.

The Barony of Letnev

Draft Order: #5

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

A powerhouse of military industrialism. They have massive fleets and the resources to sustain them. Their ships are hard to kill and they can spend trade goods to hits harder in combat.

The Ghosts of Creuss

Draft Order: #6

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

The most mobile faction in the game. They play with wormholes, creating their own exits across the galaxy. They are slippery, unpredictable, and start in a unique home system off the main board.

The Xxcha Kingdom

Draft Order: #7

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

Peace-loving turtles with giant cannons. They excel at political control and defensive "PDS" fire. They can use their flagship to shoot into neighboring systems, making them very hard to invade.

Sardakk N’orr

Draft Order: #8

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

The ultimate bug swarm. The Sardakk N’orr don't start with any technology, but they get a constant +1 bonus to all their combat rolls. They are a "brute force" faction that wins by overwhelming opponents with sheer numbers and aggressive expansion.

The Last Bastion

Draft Order: #9

Faction Reference

Assigned Map Slice

Faction Description

A unique addition to our draft. The Last Bastion represents a stalwart defensive force. Players choosing this faction should focus on securing their borders and utilizing their unique faction abilities to outlast the competition in the late game.
